## Core

### Operator ordering as an emergent gauge field in twisted bilayer graphene: singular spectral signatures at the magic angle
C. A. S. Almeida  ·  cond-mat.mes-hall  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17349
Why: Theory paper for magic-angle TBG predicting an emergent Aharonov-Bohm flux of h/(2e) at AB/BA stacking points from Hermitian operator-ordering corrections, yielding a ~171 meV Van Hove splitting absent from the standard BM spectrum — directly in moiré/flat-band territory and adjacent to the Aharonov-Casher line of work.

### Kohn--Luttinger Superconductivity in Flat Chern Bands
Nianlong Zou, Cheng Xu, Yafis Barlas, Yang Zhang  ·  cond-mat.mes-hall  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17205
Why: Establishes a geometry-driven Kohn-Luttinger mechanism in flat Chern bands where Landau-level-like form factors select chiral p- and f-wave pairing without invoking spin fluctuations, directly relevant to flat-band / moiré superconductivity and the quantum geometry of Landau-level analogs.

### How twist angle inhomogeneity masks the BKT transition and the order parameter symmetry
Ilaria Maccari, Louk Rademaker, Giulia Venditti  ·  cond-mat.supr-con  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17191
Why: Shows that correlated disorder from twist-angle inhomogeneity in moiré superconductors smears the BKT transition into a percolative one and can obscure nodal vs. gapped order-parameter symmetry — squarely in the user's moiré superconductivity interest area.
