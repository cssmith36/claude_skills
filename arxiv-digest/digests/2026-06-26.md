### Is Variational Monte Carlo Robust? Sharp Moment Thresholds and Heavy-tailed Stochastic Optimization
Philipp Grohs, Davide Nobile  ·  cs.LG  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26009
Why: Proves that VMC gradient estimators (including those used by FermiNet) are generically heavy-tailed due to nodal-set geometry, then introduces PS-Clip-VMC — a provably convergent robust variant — with preliminary FermiNet experiments on atoms up to 18 electrons showing substantially improved robustness over standard methods. Directly relevant as an NQS training/optimization methods paper.

### Two-dimensional Hyperbolic RNN Neural Quantum State
H. L. Dao  ·  quant-ph  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25600
Why: Constructs and benchmarks 2D hyperbolic (Lorentz) RNN neural quantum states for the 2D Transverse Field Ising Model, finding that hyperbolic NQS definitively outperform Euclidean NQS at the phase transition point where the physics is described by a CFT dual to hyperbolic (AdS) geometry. A new NQS architecture paper with strong empirical motivation from the CFT–AdS connection.
