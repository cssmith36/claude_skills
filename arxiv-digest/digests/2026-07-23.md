# arXiv digest — 2026-07-23    (4 core, 3 relevant)

## Core
### Enhanced NQS via Annealed Gradient Descent
Shiwei Zhou, Yiming Huang, Xiao Yuan, Xiaoxia Cai  ·  quant-ph  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18865
Why: Introduces annealed gradient descent, an NQS training/optimization fix for a finite-sample "subspace trapping" instability in stochastic VMC, tested on molecular systems and 1D/2D J1-J2 models — a core NQS-methods contribution.

### Equivariant Continuous Normalizing Flows with Offline Sampling for Fermionic Ground State Estimation
Sam Cochran, James B. Larsen, …, Chad Rigetti, Shravan Veerapaneni  ·  quant-ph  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18486
Why: Real-space fermionic VMC framework using permutation-equivariant continuous normalizing flows (including FermiNet-style vector fields) to refine Slater/Jastrow-Slater bases, with offline sampling for scalable neural-wavefunction training.

### Theory of two-dimensional Wigner crystals with defects: Interactions, melting transitions and collective modes
Paweł Matus, Tobias Holder  ·  cond-mat.str-el  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18477
Why: Builds a field-theoretic framework for vacancies, dislocations and disclinations in 2D Wigner crystals, covering defect-driven melting transitions and collective modes — directly in the Wigner-crystal/2DEG core interest.

### Quantized heat flow in moiré chern bands of bilayer graphene
Santanu Samai, Debangan Sarkar, …, Saurabh Kumar Srivastav, Anindya Das  ·  cond-mat.mes-hall  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19205
Why: Measures quantized thermal conductance of quantum Hall, Chern insulator, and interaction-driven symmetry-broken Chern insulator states in a bilayer-graphene/hBN moiré superlattice — squarely in the moiré/quantum-Hall-family core interest.

## Also relevant
### Conductivity of the Landau levels of two-dimensional Dirac cones and gapped nodal-rings in the quantum limit under impurity-potentials
Ipsita Mandal, Sanskar Sharma  ·  cond-mat.mes-hall  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18769
Why: Computes Landau-level magnetotransport (Kubo-Bastin) for 2D Dirac cones and gapped nodal rings under impurity scattering — adjacent to the quantum-Hall/Landau-level family, though single-particle and not NQS.

### A unified tight-binding description of the electronic structure and Ising protection of superconductivity in misfit layered compounds
G. A. Bobkov, I. A. Shvets, I. V. Bobkova  ·  cond-mat.supr-con  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19308
Why: Tight-binding + Bogoliubov-de Gennes model of TMD-based misfit layered compounds showing Ising-protected superconductivity via emergent interlayer spin-orbit coupling — unconventional 2D-material superconductivity without an NQS/ML angle.

### Photon Correlation Spectroscopy as a Probe of Critical Fluctuations in Correlated Electron Systems
Natasha Kiper, Bertrand Evrard, …, Takashi Taniguchi, Atac Imamoglu  ·  cond-mat.str-el  ·  https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19295
Why: Uses photon correlation spectroscopy to probe critical density fluctuations at an Ising-type layer pseudo-spin transition in a MoSe2/hBN moiré bilayer — an experimental probe of correlated electrons in a 2D moiré material.
